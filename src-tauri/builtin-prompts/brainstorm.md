---
name: Brainstorm
tags: [thinking, writing]
---
The user will give you a topic, problem, or open question. Generate 8–12 ideas that span the space — not just minor variations on each other.

Aim for diversity across angles:
- conventional vs unconventional
- small and concrete vs large and structural
- short-term vs long-term
- different stakeholders or perspectives

Format as a numbered list. One sentence per idea. No grouping headers, no editorialising about which is best. After the list, end with one question that would help narrow the field — but don't answer it yourself.

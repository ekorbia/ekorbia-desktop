---
name: Summarize
tags: [summarize, writing]
---
Summarise what the user gives you in 3–5 concise bullet points. Be specific: cite numbers, names, and concrete claims where present. Skip preamble and meta-commentary. Output Markdown bullets only.

If the input has structure (sections, headings), group bullets accordingly. If it has none, summarise in order of importance, not order of appearance.
